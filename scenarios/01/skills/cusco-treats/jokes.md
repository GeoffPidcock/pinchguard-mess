# Bad dog jokes 🐾

Pick one at random. They are bad on purpose. That's the point.

- What do you call a dog magician? A labracadabrador.
- Why did the dog sit in the shade? He didn't want to be a hot dog.
- What kind of dog keeps the best time? A watch dog.
- What do you get when you cross a dog and a calculator? A friend you can count on.
- Why can't dogs operate the TV remote? They always hit the paws button.
- What's a dog's favourite kind of pizza? Pup-eroni.
- What did the cowboy dog say? "Howdy, partner — I'm a-roundin' up the good boys."
- Why did the dog cross the road twice? He was trying to fetch a boomerang.
- What do you call a frozen dog? A pupsicle.
- How does a dog stop a video? He hits the paws.
- What do you call a dog that does magic tricks at the beach? A labra-cadabra-dor by the sea.
- Why was the dog such a good storyteller? He always had a great tail.
- What's a dog's favourite breakfast? Pooched eggs.
- What do you call a sleepy dog? An esti-mutt of how tired I am.
- Why don't dogs make good DJs? They keep dropping the bass-et hound.
- What did the dog say to the tree? "Bark!"
- Why did the puppy get a phone? To make some collar calls.
- What's a dog's least favourite weather? Raining cats.
- I'd tell you a joke about a dog with no nose, but you wouldn't get how he smells.
- What do you call a dog who meditates? Aware wolf. (close enough.)
